Account Deletion Request / Permintaan Penghapusan Akun

Last updated: 6 June 2026

SOOVA users may request account deletion through the mobile application or by contacting SOOVA support.

Pengguna SOOVA dapat meminta penghapusan akun melalui aplikasi mobile atau dengan menghubungi dukungan SOOVA.

1. Delete Account from the App

When available, users can delete their account from:

Settings → Account → Delete Account

2. External Deletion Request

If users cannot access the application, they may request deletion by email.

Request Account Deletion

3. Required Information

Please include registered email address, username, full name, and reason for deletion. SOOVA may request verification to protect account security.

4. Data Removed

Account profile, contact information, preferences, wishlist, and non-essential user data may be deleted.

5. Data Retained

SOOVA may retain limited data required for legal compliance, transaction records, dispute handling, fraud prevention, safety, or platform integrity.

6. Processing Time

Account deletion requests are processed within a reasonable period after verification.
