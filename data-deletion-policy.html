Data Deletion Policy / Kebijakan Penghapusan Data

Last updated: 6 June 2026

This policy explains how users may request deletion of personal data from SOOVA.

Kebijakan ini menjelaskan cara pengguna dapat meminta penghapusan data pribadi dari SOOVA.

1. Data That May Be Deleted

SOOVA may delete account profile data, contact information, travel preferences, wishlist data, reviews where legally permitted, chat data where applicable, and non-essential usage data.

2. Data That May Be Retained

SOOVA may retain limited records when necessary for legal compliance, fraud prevention, dispute resolution, security, booking history, financial records, safety, or platform integrity.

3. Account Deletion

Users may request account deletion through the SOOVA application or through the external account deletion request page.

4. Processing Time

Deletion requests are processed within a reasonable period after identity verification. Some data may remain in backups for a limited retention period before permanent removal.

5. Service Provider and Destination Manager Data

Business records, public service listings, transaction records, verified tourism data, and destination management records may require additional review before deletion.

6. Contact

Email: support@soova.app
