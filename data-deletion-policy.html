Data Deletion Policy

Last updated: 6 June 2026

This policy explains how users may request deletion of personal data from SOOVA.

Data That May Be Deleted

SOOVA may delete profile data, contact information, travel preferences, wishlist data, reviews where legally permitted, chat data where applicable, and non-essential usage data.

Data That May Be Retained

SOOVA may retain limited records for legal compliance, fraud prevention, dispute resolution, security, financial records, booking history, or platform integrity.

Processing Time

Deletion requests are processed within a reasonable period after verification.
