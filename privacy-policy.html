Privacy Policy

Last updated: 6 June 2026

SOOVA respects user privacy and protects personal data used in the SOOVA Android application.

Information We Collect

SOOVA may collect name, username, email, phone number, profile information, travel preferences, booking data, reviews, chat data, location data, and app usage data.

How We Use Information

Data is used for authentication, profiles, destination discovery, booking, chat, maps, recommendations, notifications, analytics, security, and support.

Location Data

Location data may be used to show nearby destinations, routes, provider locations, meeting points, and location-based recommendations.

Data Sharing

SOOVA does not sell personal data. Data may be shared only when required for app functionality, legal compliance, security, or tourism service delivery.
