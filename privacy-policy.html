Privacy Policy / Kebijakan Privasi

Last updated: 6 June 2026

SOOVA respects user privacy and is committed to protecting personal data used within the SOOVA Android application.

SOOVA menghormati privasi pengguna dan berkomitmen untuk melindungi data pribadi yang digunakan dalam aplikasi Android SOOVA.

1. Information We Collect

SOOVA may collect full name, username, email address, phone number, profile photo, country, travel interests, location data, booking data, reviews, chat data, device information, and app usage data.

2. How We Use Information

Data is used for authentication, user profiles, destination discovery, booking, chat, maps, location-based recommendations, itinerary planning, notifications, analytics, security, and support.

3. Location Data

SOOVA may use location data to show nearby destinations, provider locations, meeting points, travel routes, distance estimation, and location-based recommendations. Users may manage location permission through device settings.

4. Firebase Services

SOOVA may use Firebase Authentication, Cloud Firestore, Firebase Storage, Firebase Cloud Messaging, and Firebase Cloud Functions to operate the application securely.

5. Data Sharing

SOOVA does not sell personal data. Data may be shared only when required for app functionality, service delivery, legal compliance, safety, fraud prevention, or communication between tourists, service providers, and destination managers.

6. Data Retention

User data is retained as long as necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, maintain transaction records, and protect platform integrity.

7. User Rights

Users may request access, correction, or deletion of personal data through the application or by contacting SOOVA support.

8. Children’s Privacy

SOOVA is not intended for users below the minimum legal age required by applicable law.

9. Contact

Email: support@soova.app
